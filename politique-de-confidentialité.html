COOKIE POLICY


Gammapps (“us”, “we”, or “our”) uses cookies on www.gammapps.com (the “Service”). By using the Service, you consent to the use of cookies. Our Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use cookies, how third parties we may partner with may use cookies on the Service, your choices regarding cookies, and further information about cookies. This Cookie Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.

WHAT ARE COOKIES

Cookies are small text files stored on and accessed from your device when you use our Service. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the Service or a third-party to recognize you and make your next visit easier and the Service more useful to you. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies.

HOW GAMMAPPS USES COOKIES

When you use and access the Service, we may place a number of cookie files in your web browser. We use cookies for the following purposes: to enable certain functions of the Service, to provide analytics, to store your preferences, and to enable advertisement delivery, including targeted advertising. We use both persistent and session cookies. Persistent cookies are used to improve a user’s experience on the site; an example of a persistent cookie is recording your acceptance of our Cookie Policy to remove the cookie message which first appears when you use the Service. Session cookies are temporary and are deleted from your machine after the browser is closed – session cookies are used to track internet usage as described above.

THIRD-PARTY COOKIES

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the Service, deliver advertisements on and through the Service, and so on.

WHAT ARE YOUR CHOICES REGARDING COOKIES

If you'd like to delete cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows you to decline their use and to alert you when a cookie is placed on your device. If you want to remove previously stored cookies, you can manually delete the cookies at any time. However, this will not prevent the Service from placing further cookies on your device unless and until you adjust your Internet browser setting as described above. Please note, however, that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, some or all of the features, functionality available through the Gammapps website − such as the online chat, the ability to view certain pages – may not be available to you.

WHERE CAN YOU FIND MORE INFORMATION ABOUT COOKIES
